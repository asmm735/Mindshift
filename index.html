Track your mood!

A mood tracker is a tool that helps individuals monitor and record their emotional states over time. It can be a simple app, a journal, or even a spreadsheet. While it might seem trivial at first, tracking your mood can have significant benefits for mental health, self-awareness, and personal growth.

Why use a Pomodoro Timer?

Improves Focus: Breaking work into intervals helps maintain concentration. Prevents Burnout: Regular breaks prevent mental fatigue. Boosts Productivity: Structured work sessions encourage task completion. Encourages Time Management: Helps you estimate how long tasks take. Reduces Procrastination: The timer creates a sense of urgency.
